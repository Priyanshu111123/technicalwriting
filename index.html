Predictive Noise Cancellation System

1. Purpose of the Invention

In today’s fast-paced, noise-filled world, maintaining a calm and focused environment—whether at home, in the office, or in public spaces—has become increasingly difficult. The Predictive Noise Cancellation System aims to solve this by using machine learning to anticipate disruptive sounds and take intelligent actions like adjusting speaker output, injecting calming white noise, or suppressing interference.

Example: You're on a video call and road construction begins outside. Your smart speaker boosts voice clarity and masks the external noise without you lifting a finger.

2. Technical Working

  • Sound Detection: Microphones in smart speakers or IoT devices continuously monitor the environment.
  • Noise Classification and Prediction: A machine learning model processes audio signals, learning and predicting patterns over time.
  • Adaptive Response:
    • Plays white noise during heavy construction.
    • Pauses or lowers volume during conversations.
    • Boosts dialog clarity in noisy environments.
  • User Preference Integration: Learns from behaviors such as volume changes during workouts or conversations.
  • Hybrid Intelligence: Performs most tasks locally with occasional cloud updates for improvement.

3. Distinguishing Features

  • Proactive, Not Reactive: Predicts and prepares for recurring noise.
  • Context Awareness: Treats different noise types accordingly.
  • Custom Sound Profiles: Individual preferences for each user.
  • Zoned Control: Room-specific noise control and behavior.
  • Privacy & Efficiency: On-device processing ensures data safety.
  • Smart Integration: Compatible with Alexa, Google Home, Siri, and routines.

A. Problem It Solves

  1. Distractions During Remote Work or Study
    Neighbor starts mowing. System injects white noise and boosts call audio clarity.
  2. Sleep Disruption Due to Environmental Noise
    Thunderstorm starts. System plays pink noise to maintain restful sleep.
  3. Stress and Anxiety from Urban Living
    Construction noise outside. System plays relaxing ambient sounds and reduces disturbance.
  4. Communication Interference in Social Settings
    During conversation, music volume is automatically reduced and restored later.
  5. Sound Overlap in Shared Spaces
    Keeps bedroom quiet during a sibling’s online class while managing movie sound in living room.
  6. Difficulty for People with Sensory Sensitivities
    Creates calming environment during outdoor party noise for a sensory-sensitive child.

B. Objective of the Invention

  • Enhance Daily Comfort: Smart audio adaptation without manual effort.
  • Improve Focus & Productivity: Reduce distractions in work and study environments.
  • Support Better Sleep: Automatically mask disruptive nighttime noises.
  • Adapt to Preferences: Learn and tailor to each person’s sound habits.
  • Promote Accessibility: Aid users with sensory sensitivity through personalized soundscapes.
  • Smart Ecosystem Integration: Easily works with existing smart devices and routines.
